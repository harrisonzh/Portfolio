Project Description

Safety is the first priority in the workplace, and submit incident reports will help reduce incidents in the workplace and improve workplace safety.
According to the research done by Dr.Millet. In the past, most incident reports were completed by using digital systems, and near miss reports were completed by using the printed form and verbal reports, and those report forms faced many problems, including:

  • Type of data collected (mostly narrative)- Yields insufficient and inaccurate data;
  • Too time consuming;
  • Little opportunity to conduct quantitative analyses for quality improvement;
  • Practical problems in printed form: forms not easily obtained and staff must be motivated to find one, fill it in, and then submit to the appropriate manager;

MedicGuard is a webbased error reporting system for nursing home employees, the tools I used to design this system were Adobe illustrator and Miro for the mind map, concept map, sitemap, and task flow, Adobe XD for prototyping. Designed for a Human-Centered Design (HCD) class, Fall 2020.

Requirements

  • Used mostly on desktop.
  • System access via login.
  • View blank forms and review submitted forms (by all users).
  • Complete and submit forms (for self and other staff).
  • Save form to complete and submit later (for self and other staff).
  • Show confirmation with report # for submitted forms; submitted by and date tracked by system.
  • Repository of incomplete and submitted forms.
  • Append an incident case with administrator review and additional (associated) forms.

Product Description

MedicGuard is an error reporting system designed for the nursing home employees and administrators. The system has a dashboard page to show the recent activity in the workplace. Users are able to creating new reports, submitting reports, editing in progress reports, reviewing submitted reports, appending case documentations, populate OSHA/ Workers Comp forms on the system. The system will also determine a safety score of the work place according to the recent incidents and shows on the dashboard. MedicGuard error reporting system is also committed to reducing accidents through dashboard analysis.
