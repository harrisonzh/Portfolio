Hi, I'm Haoran Zhang.

I am a UX Designer.

I recently completed an intensive, two-year STEM-based graduate program from the University of Miami, School of Communications earning a Master's degree in Interactive Media with a focus on UX Research and Interaction Design.
To me, a design is a discipline of rationality and sensibility. I tend to see the positive and valuable parts of everything to maintain my creative thinking and eliminate the complexity of my design problems.
My interest in digital art and design has helped developing my own design thinking. I tend to learn from every design and designers to gain the ability to transform work from concepts into reality. My sense of design has a high degree of enthusiasm, also through different levels of training, with the ability to apply computer programs and creative design concepts.
