Hi, I'm Haoran Zhang.

I am a UX/UI Designer.

I am currently an interactive media graduate student from an intensive, two-year STEM-based graduate program from the University of Miami, School of Communications with a focus on UX Research and Interaction Design. Projected graduation date in May 2021.

To me, a design is a discipline of rationality and sensibility. I tend to see the positive and valuable parts of everything to maintain my creative thinking and eliminate the complexity of my design problems.

My interest in user experience design has helped developing my own design thinking. I tend to learn from every design and designers to gain the ability to transform work from concepts into reality. My sense of design has a high degree of enthusiasm, also through different levels of training, with the ability to apply computer programs and creative design concepts.
