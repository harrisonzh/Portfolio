For this week’s progress, I work on competitor analysis, personas, storyboard.

Competitive analysis

I found three competitors, including Civilisations AR, Acropolis Interactive Educational 3D, and ARki: A-R Architecture.

Civilisations AR

Civilisations AR App is an AR app made by BBC, users can enjoy precious historical relics from all over the world through mobile phones. This app includes about 30 3D models of historical relics. Users can put the relics in front of them and rotate them, take photos, and even use x-ray to see what's inside the sarcophagus. Civilisations AR App is also my design precedents and highly inspired me.

Pro:

  • Civilisations AR is a product of cooperation between the BBC and more than 30 museums in the UK. The model is extremely realistic and very similar to the real exhibits. The functions of x-ray make people feel immersive, and the AR environment brings a good experience to users.
  • The rotation function of the 3D model allows users to observe the exhibits from multiple angles, and users can also see a brief introduction of each exhibit, that make users feel like they really in a museum.

Cons:

  • For the products that the BBC has collaborated with more than 30 museums, 30 models are too few.
  • Not every exhibit has an audio introduction.
  • The tutorial is not clear enough for people who are not familiar with AR.
  • Not work on Android 9.


Acropolis Interactive Educational 3D

Acropolis Interactive Educational 3D is an educational APP dedicated to explaining the history of the Acropolis castle in Athens, Greece in the 5th century BC. Users can turn on the VR mode and use VR headsets to tour the historical sites.

Pro:

  • The VR function makes people feel immersive.
  • Users are able to interact with many buildings, and users can see the analysis of the building.
  • The voice over function makes me feel like I am really traveling in Athens, and the tour guide is introducing me to the history and culture of Athens.
  • There is a walk mode in this app, users can explore and walk in the city of Athens.

Cons:

  • The accuracy of the model is not high enough, and it does not resemble the real building itself.
  • There are some wall-clipping issues in this app.
  • The adaptation to different devices is not good, the scene distorted on my phone.


ARki: A-R Architecture

ARki is an AR app for architectural models. This app can display the architecture model in the real world through AR technology.

Pro:

  • ARki provides lots of interactive functionalities, users can adjust the sunlight, the color of the building, etc. Users can also record videos through ARki.
  • Users are able to import their own model to ARki, and ARki supports lots of model files, including maya, 3dmax, skp, c4d, etc.
  • The app is very easy to use.

Cons:

  • Not very user-friendly for non-architect designers.
  • If you don't have your own model, this will be a very boring app. Because the user can only view the 6 models included in the app, the user cannot get more information except for some simple interactions with the model.
  • Scanning outdoor scenes is slow and not very accurate.
  • The UI interface looks a bit rough.

Personas

After conducting user research, I made two personas, and they are a 24-year-old travel blogger Peyton Howland, and a middle school teacher Marvin McKinney who is also a father of three children. I was tring to restore the people I met during user research and think about their needs during the design process.

Story Board

1. Fiona hasn’t traveled for a long time because of coronavirus. He recently watched an Chinese movie and wanted to see the historic buildings in China. But because of the travel ban, he could not go to China. He found landmark AR app on the Internet, and he wanted to try it.
2. Fiona tried to use the Landmark AR app at home, and then she found that she could use this app to view a full-scale landmark.
3. Fiona took the mobile phone and walked inside the building to experience the full range, just like being on the scene.
4. She can also use the app to read related information about the building, and she was very happy.
