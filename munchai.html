Product Description

Munch AI is an AI app that will identify what’s on the plate and calculate the percentage of the types of food group observed (protein, fat, carb...etc). It will then show how much of the daily nutrient requirement has been reached. After that, the app will give suggestions on what your next meal should be so you get all the nutrients you need in a day to sustain a healthy, nutritious diet.
We will focus on popular fast-food restaurants, including Shake Shack, McDonald's, Wendy’s, etc. Because that will help us to find clear nutrition information on their menu items.
The tools we used to design this AI application were Adobe illustrator and Miro for the mind map, concept map and personas, Adobe XD for prototyping and Xcode developer tool for testing and training the model for image recognition. Designed for a Design with AI class, Fall 2020.

Goal

  • Helps users to know more about what they eat.
  • Allow people to track their nutrient intake.
  • Helps users to get a well balanced diet.
  • Next meal suggestions based on users' nutrient intake.

Features and Functions

  • Identify food by image recognition.
  • Next meal suggestions.
  • Show nutrient content of the food.
  • Calculate daily nutrient requirement.
  • Show how much of the daily nutrient requirement has been reached.
  • Add a meal by user input.
  • Set users' height and weight.
  • View meal history.

Persona

From the list of features we sketched out the rough idea of how we want to have our app to look like. Next we created two personas to help us identify our target user group. Our target user group would be for people who want to eat healthy and keep track of their calories intake. They are interested in learning more about the nutrient content in the food they eat as well as getting next meal suggestions. The feature of providing next meal suggestion is an example of anticipatory design that improves the quality of decision making. It is a method of simplifying processes to the needs of users one step ahead of the user’s decision.

User Testing

We wrote a usability test script and did four usability tests using our prototype. Below are the tasks on our usability test script.

  1. Check meal history.
  2. Find where you can set your height and weight
  3. Add a meal from shake shack.
  4. Use the flashlight to take the image
  5. Get next meal suggestion
  6. Take a picture of your meal.
